Sonim XP7 Quad-core KitKat 4G NFC PTT IP68 & IP69 Ultra-rugged smartphone rated IP68 and IP69, Quad-core with Android KitKat, NFC, LTE(4G) connectivity, PTT and a Single SIM. Sonim XP7: Ultra-rugged smartphone, it is built with hardened rubber molded housing and can withstand up to 4 J of impact energy. Its Corning Gorilla Glass screen can withstand impacts of up to 2 J. It is dustproof and waterproof rated IP68 (complete submergibility in water up to 2 m for 30 minutes) and IP69 (for resistance to high temperature pressure wash) and it can operate at temperatures between -20°C and +55°C. It will withstand up to a ton of metric pressure, to sudden shocks of up to 30G, to vibrations from 5 Hz to 500 Hz and to 2 meters drops onto concrete from any angle. It is also resistant to petroleum oils and the corrosive nature of cleaning solvents. It is compatible with Bell and TELUS 4G LTE data networks in Canada.
