Samsung DA-E750 Audio Dock How to connect to the Audio Dock wirelessly? ( Through a JBC - JukeBox Connection ) Note: The device works only with WPA2-PSK --> AES and with 2.4GHz So, in your router you setup the wireless WPA2 Personal --> AES As shown here 1.iMAC wireless Settings: 1.Turn the Wi-Fi ON ( you don't have to turn Wired OFF. It will be in a different subnet ) 2.Network Name: Click and choose the Samsung W_Audio E750 1.If you don't see it in the list, click on Join Other Network and then click on Show Networks 2.Go to http://192.168.1.1 and setup the device according to your network.
